Vision Statement

I strive to become a leader in computer science, at the forefront of technological advancement, applying my knowledge to tackle challenging issues and produce answers that enhance the lives of individuals, strengthen communities, and help build a more connected, sustainable world.

Mission Statement

My mission is to utilize my computer science expertise to create, develop, and implement effective solutions that tackle problems in the real world. In addition, I aim to encourage morality in the use of technology and to motivate the next generation of computer scientists.


Desired Career: Developer Analyst

Who is a Developer Analyst?
A Developer Analyst helps a company figure out what it needs in terms of software or technology and then works to build, improve, or maintain those solutions. They understand the general flow of business operations and how technology can solve problems, making them a valuable bridge between the business side and the technical side.

Requirements:

  1. Software Development: Must be able to develop, debug and maintain software applications.

  2. Testing: Conduct various types of software testing and work with QA teams to ensure high-quality deliverables.

  3. Business Analysis: Analyze and understand business work flows and propose appropriate solutions.

  4. Collaborative Work: Work closely with business analysts, project managers, and developers to meet objectives.

  5. Project Management Support: Assist in project planning and tracking and reporting of project progress.


Qualifications:

  • Minimum Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering or a related technical field.

  • Optional degree or certifications in fields related to business analysis.

  • Optional Programming Certifications.

  • 2-5 years of experience in software development, analysis or a similar role.

  • Familiarity with the following:

    • specific programming languages and frameworks (Java, C, Python, Spring boot, React, etc.).

    • database management and system architecture (various relational databases and cloud services).

    • project management concepts, software and tools (Agile, Scrum, Kanban, Jira).

    • DevOps principles, tools and software (GitHub, Kubernetes, Octopus).


Skills:

  • Adaptability - Open to learning new technologies and adapting to changing business environments.

  • Time management - Ability to handle multiple projects and meet deadlines.

  • Interpersonal skills - Able to communicate and work effectively in a team. Can collaborate with others outside the technical role including project managers and stakeholders.

  • Analytical - General problem solving mindset to optimize processes and able to understand business requirements, providing technical solutions.
